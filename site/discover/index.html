Are you new to Shamanic Energy Practices?

Please watch the Q&A video first to learn more about the many ways we can work together.

If you have a unique life situation, or you simply feel that you need to share aspects of your story with another person, a Discovery Call is the best way to start this journey. During the private and confidential call, you may ask questions, share life experiences, and discuss your desire to allow any negative feelings that you would like to be released in a session.

Please use the calendar below to schedule.

You are safe to share anything that is on your mind, even something deeply personal, whatever you’d like to discuss with the Shaman is 100% confidential.

Important Details Before Scheduling
This 30-minute discovery call is complimentary. No energy medicine or healing work is performed during this conversation. It is a time to explore whether Shamanic Energy Healing is aligned for you.

All future Shamanic Energy Healing sessions are paid in full at the time of scheduling.

Please note: John begins energetic preparation before each scheduled call. If you are unable to attend, you agree to cancel or reschedule in advance using the link provided. Missed appointments without notice are not in alignment with the integrity of this work and may prevent future complimentary sessions from being offered.

Appointments scheduled less than 48 hours in advance are not confirmed.
If you prefer to begin with a direct conversation, you are welcome to start a text message exchange with John Whitehorse at +1 (505) 660-9711.
Text or WhatsApp available.

We look forward to connecting with you.

So if you are ready to discover the path to your enlightenment, wholeness, and develop a deep sense of satisfaction in your new life, let’s connect!
