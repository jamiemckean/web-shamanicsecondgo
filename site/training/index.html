Shamanic Training in the Andean Tradition

A Sacred Initiation into the Living Lineage of the Earthkeepers

You May Feel the Calling…

Not all will be called to this path.
But if you’re still reading, something ancient within you is stirring.
A whisper in your blood.
A knowing in your bones.
A remembrance that you have walked this way before.

This is a rare and sacred initiation into a pure, unbroken lineage of Andean shamanism—one that has been protected, preserved, and passed orally from elder to elder for generations.

Until now, it has never been fully shared with the Western world.

A Disappearing Tradition

The Q’ero shamans of the High Andes are among the last living descendants of the Inka priesthood. Their medicine is not learned—it is lived. Their wisdom is not theoretical—it is energetic, embodied, and held in ceremony, fire, and breath.

Many of these wisdom keepers are now elders. Their time is sacred. Their teachings precious. And their readiness to share is rare.

This is a closing window.
The tradition still lives—but only just in this precious moment.

The Now…

A Lineage Now Carried Forward

A Lineage Now
Carried Forward

John Whitehorse, now recognized as a shaman elder in this tradition, and his wife Kristen White, are among the very few Westerners to be fully initiated in the Q’ero lineage—directly by the Q’ero themselves.

After decades of walking the path in deep reverence and service, they have been given permission to transmit the teachings, rites, and energetic medicine in the purest form of which it has always existed.
This is not a modern interpretation.

This is direct transmission from the SOURCE.

There is no Plant Medicine.

Only sacred ceremony, deep energy work, and luminous truth carried in the mesa – each thread woven by centuries of ancestors and gifted your radiant heart.

What Makes This Path Unique

This is the real work. It is intimate, initiatory, and energetic from a powerful and pure source.

Through this rare training, you will:

  • Receive ancient rites of initiation that open spiritual vision, healing power, and earth-based wisdom
  • Learn to build and carry your mesa—your personal medicine bundle of sacred space and accumulated healing energy.
  • Participate in powerful fire ceremonies, Despacho offerings, and Direct Lineage rituals of initiation
  • Experience personal healing and soul retrieval guided by indigenous and initiated shamans
  • Learn to walk as a seer, healer, and ceremonial steward
  • Reconnect with Pachamama, the Apus (mountain spirits), and the star beings
  • Activate soul gifts encoded within you—some of which have been dormant for lifetimes
